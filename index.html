Research Foundation

Graduate-level quantitative research experience that shaped my analytical perspective.

Master’s Thesis: Life Satisfaction of Middle- and Older-Aged Retirees

Yonsei University · Panel regression · KLoSA longitudinal data

For my master's thesis at Yonsei University, I analyzed the determinants of life satisfaction among middle-aged and older retirees using panel regression with KLoSA longitudinal data. A key finding was that subjective indicators — perceived social class and self-rated health — carried greater explanatory power than objective measures, which deepened my conviction that human perception and affect must be treated as substantive analytical constructs.
